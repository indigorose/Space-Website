02Meet Your Crew

Commander

Douglas Hurley

Douglas Gerald Hurley is a United States Marine Corps officer and former NASA astronaut. He was the pilot of the final Space Shuttle mission, STS-135, and he is a veteran of two other spaceflights. He is currently the Director of Human Spaceflight at SpaceX.
